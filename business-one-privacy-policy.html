Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2025

Business One ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website, submit a contact form, or use our business services including point-of-sale systems, payment processing, phone service, and website development.

Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and business name
  • Messages and service interests submitted through our contact form
  • Account, billing, and support details related to services you request from us
  • Communications you send to us by phone, email, or other channels

We may also automatically collect limited technical information when you use our website, such as browser type, device information, pages viewed, and general usage data through standard server logs or cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide requested information about our services
  • Deliver, maintain, and support POS, payment, phone, and website solutions
  • Process transactions and manage customer relationships
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience
  • Send service-related notices and, where permitted, marketing communications
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist with hosting, communications, payment processing, or business operations
  • Professional advisors where reasonably necessary
  • Law enforcement or other parties when required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • A successor entity in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to support basic functionality and understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, provide services, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal requirements.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of certain personal information we hold about you. You may also opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in those messages or by contacting us directly.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can delete it.
